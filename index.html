When choosing your new car, you may prefer a specific Make of the car. The scatterplot visualization below will help you decide what make best fits your needs and fuel efficiency expectations.

Again, each circle represents a car and Average Highway MPG is plotted on the vertical axis and Average City MPG is plotted on the horizontal axis. The radius of the circles is proportional to the number of cylinders in each car.
